FOUNDER'S STORY

THE HEART
BEHIND LOOM SAGA

Loom Saga began as a dream woven quietly in the mind of Priya Devarapalli — a woman who grew up surrounded by the elegance, grace, and soulful artistry of handloom sarees. For Priya, a saree was never just a garment. It was a memory. A tradition. A form of self-expression that carried stories from the hands of weavers to the hearts of women.

Raised with a deep appreciation for India's textile heritage, Priya observed how the world was shifting to fast fashion, while the voices of handloom artisans grew faint.

Their skill, patience, and craft — passed down through generations — were slowly fading into shadows.

Priya felt a calling.

She believed that handloom sarees deserved a place not just in ceremonies, but in everyday life. A place where tradition met contemporary design. A place where the artistry of the loom was celebrated, preserved, and honoured.

That belief became Loom Saga.

A JOURNEY ROOTED IN PURPOSE

When Priya founded Loom Saga, her vision was clear:

Revive and preserve authentic handloom weaving

Support artisan communities through fair, ethical, and respectful practices

Craft sarees that feel timeless yet modern, suitable for powerful, graceful women of today

Create a brand that feels personal, emotional, and meaningful

Every collection reflects Priya's eye for design — minimalist, soulful, elegant — combined with the raw beauty of traditional weaving.

CRAFTING MORE THAN SAREES

Priya believes that a saree carries emotion.

The confidence of a first step.

The grace of a celebration.

The quiet power of a woman choosing to embrace her roots.

Under her leadership, Loom Saga partners with skilled weavers who bring each saree to life — thread by thread, hour by hour, with unmatched dedication. Priya spends time closely understanding their processes, designs, and challenges, ensuring that every saree represents integrity, respect, and authenticity.

A WOMAN'S VISION FOR WOMEN EVERYWHERE

For Priya, Loom Saga is not just a brand — it's a movement.

A movement that celebrates:

Slow fashion

Honest craft

Traditional artistry

The unique beauty of every woman

Her mission is to make handloom accessible, stylish, and an essential part of modern wardrobes — while staying rooted in heritage.

THE SAGA CONTINUES...

Today, Loom Saga stands as a symbol of handcrafted luxury, cultural pride, and feminine strength — all shaped lovingly under Priya Devarapalli's guidance.

Her journey is still unfolding.

Her passion continues to grow.

And through every saree, she hopes to ignite a spark — a connection between the wearer and the craft, the tradition, the story.

Because for Priya, every saree is a saga.

And every woman deserves to wear one with pride.
